Why Schedule vs Just Booking?

Scheduled Pickups are pre-arranged in advance and get priority over walk-in or same-day requests. Since TonyTaxi is one dedicated driver, scheduling ensures your spot is locked in before someone else books that time. Think of it like a reservation at a great restaurant — you can walk in, but reservations always come first.

Book a Ride (below) is for same-day or short-notice requests. Tony will confirm availability, but scheduled members always get first priority.

📞 You can still call Tony directly anytime — scheduling through the app just makes it official and locks in your time.

💳 $10 Security Deposit Required — Confirms your scheduled time slot and protects Tony's time. Refundable if cancelled 2+ hours before pickup. Non-refundable for no-shows or late cancellations.
SAME-DAY / SHORT NOTICE

🚘 Book a Ride

Need a ride soon? Submit a request and Tony will confirm availability. Scheduled pickups always take priority, but Tony will do his best to accommodate you. For guaranteed availability, use Schedule a Pickup above.

💳 A $10 deposit secures your ride and protects Tony's time. No-shows or last-minute cancellations (within 5 miles of pickup) forfeit the deposit.

VIP Membership Terms

TonyTaxi VIP Membership is a privilege-based personal service. Membership may be revoked for: no-shows or repeated last-minute cancellations, disrespectful or unsafe behavior, sharing VIP access with non-members, fraudulent bookings, or any conduct deemed harmful to the service.

The $10 deposit for scheduled rides and bookings is non-refundable for no-shows or cancellations made when Tony is within 5 miles of your pickup location. Deposits for cancellations made 2+ hours in advance may be refunded at Tony's discretion.

TonyTaxi VIP operates under ZTrip and is powered by High Prosperity Apps. Service availability is based on scheduling and driver availability. This is not a guaranteed public transportation service.
